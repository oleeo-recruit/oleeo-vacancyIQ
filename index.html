How VacancyIQ works

From rough role idea to polished job pack.

VacancyIQ gives busy teams a simple, guided route from role requirements to content that is clear, consistent, inclusive and ready to review.

1

Tell VacancyIQ about the role

Enter the job title, level, key duties, must-have requirements and any important context in plain language.

2

Generate the full job pack

Produce the advert, description, person specification, behaviours and application or interview questions in one place.

3

Review, refine and share

Quickly adjust tone, level and emphasis, then paste into your ATS, job boards, briefing notes or interview packs.
